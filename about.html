Who We Are

RajVastra was founded in August 2025 by Banti Kempal, a passionate entrepreneur from Jalore, Rajasthan. Our brand is dedicated to bringing traditional Rajasthani culture and craftsmanship into modern fashion, while maintaining authenticity, quality, and elegance.

At RajVastra, every outfit tells a story — a story of culture, tradition, hard work, and pride. We work closely with skilled artisans and handloom craftsmen to ensure that our products reflect purity, originality, and excellence. Our goal is not just to sell clothes, but to promote the rich heritage of Rajasthan and provide customers with designs that make them feel royal, confident, and connected to our culture.

RajVastra was founded in August 2025 by Banti Kempal, a dedicated and passionate entrepreneur from Jalore, Rajasthan. RajVastra was created with a simple yet powerful vision — to bring the beauty, elegance, and cultural pride of Rajasthani traditional wear to people everywhere. Growing up in the vibrant land of Rajasthan, I (Banti Kempal) have always admired the colors, patterns, and artistry that define our heritage. I saw talented local artisans crafting beautiful traditional clothes, yet their art often remained unseen and unappreciated. So, I decided to create RajVastra — a brand that supports artisans, preserves traditional craftsmanship, and brings authentic ethnic fashion into the modern world. At RajVastra, every outfit tells a story — a story of culture, tradition, hard work, and pride. We work closely with skilled artisans and handloom craftsmen to ensure that our products reflect purity, originality, and excellence. Our goal is not just to sell clothes, but to promote the rich heritage of Rajasthan and provide customers with designs that make them feel royal, confident, and connected to our culture. RajVastra is more than a brand — it is a representation of art, identity, and tradition. Thank you for being a part of our journey. Together, we celebrate culture, creativity, and the timeless beauty of Rajasthan.
